Black Adam

Black Adam is an upcoming American superhero film based on the DC Comics character of the same name. Produced by New Line Cinema, DC Films, Seven Bucks Productions, and FlynnPictureCo., and set for distribution by Warner Bros.

Darkseid

Darkseid is a supervillain appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. Created by writer-artist Jack Kirby, the character briefly appeared in Superman's Pal Jimmy Olsen #134 in December, 1970, before being fully introduced in the debut issue of The Forever People in February, 1971.
